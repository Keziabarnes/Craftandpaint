About Craft & Paint With Us JA

Where Jamaican creativity meets wearable art — one brushstroke at a time.

Jamaica's Premier Paint & Sip Experience

Craft & Paint With Us JA was born out of a love for art, community, and the vibrant spirit of Jamaica. We bring people together to paint, sip, laugh, and leave with a one-of-a-kind hand-crafted tote bag.

Whether you're a seasoned artist or have never touched a paintbrush, our expert guides make sure everyone walks away proud of what they created.

Our Mission

To inspire creativity and celebrate Jamaican culture through guided paint-and-sip experiences that produce wearable, handcrafted art.

Our Vision

To be Jamaica's most beloved creative lifestyle brand, empowering every person to discover their inner artist.

Our Craft

Premium acrylic paints, high-quality canvas totes, and expert guidance in a fun, relaxed atmosphere.

Our Community

Proudly Jamaican, deeply community-focused. We celebrate local talent and our island's rich culture.
